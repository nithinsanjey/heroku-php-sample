Lesson from Engineering mathematics 1

January 23, 2018

After 2 years of state board education reading the same book over and over again, going to college was different. I would have listened to only very few technical classes in my school/college. I am not proud of not listening, but I liked discussions of Mr T.V.Gopal which I listened. But somehow I tried and could not listen to most other topics.

Kokilavani madam handled our Engineering mathematics paper. I hear my friends complain a lot about her, but I never understood it as I had only minimum clue of what was happening in the class. But I really liked the questions that she had chosen for our first assessment. They needed broader and in-depth thinking. I felt good taking that test.

But in successive assessments such questions never came, maybe because some students would have persuaded her to take easier questions.

But now I feel that I should have expressed my feeling to her that that the first assessment questions were good for me and should have at-least have personally asked for similar question to workout for myself.
